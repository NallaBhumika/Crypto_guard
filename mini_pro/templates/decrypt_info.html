__Decrypt__

Decryption is a process that transforms encrypted information into its original format.

Caesar cipher is an algorithm in cryptography that takes two inputs a number and a text. It decrypted the text with the help of a number.

  • The formula of Caesar cipher decryption is De(x) = (x - n) mod 26.
  • De(x) stands for Decryption of x
  • n is the number that is going to be added
  • mod 26 is the modulus
